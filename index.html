About Me

I am a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Georgia Tech. My research interests include diffusion models, inverse problems, out-of-distribution detection, and machine learning for imaging systems.

Research

My current work focuses on localized distribution shift detection using diffusion priors in inverse problems, with applications in medical imaging and computer vision.
